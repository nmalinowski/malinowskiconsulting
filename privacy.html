Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how Malinowski Consulting, LLC handles information collected through our website.

Information Collection

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide through our contact forms, such as your name, email address, and message details. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiries and provide requested services.

Analytics

We use PostHog to collect lightweight, privacy-focused analytics that help us measure site performance and usage. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users personally. You may opt out via your browser's privacy settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or lease your personal data to third parties. Form submissions are processed through secure third-party services like Web3Forms for delivery to our team.
